Joshua D. Starkey is a highly motivated, front end-focused Software Engineer with a background in science, technology and writing. He is passionate about learning new technologies and understanding how to use them to create solutions for real-life problems.

Josh specializes in front end web development, but also has a strong understanding of back end technologies. He is proficient in responsive web design, A/B testing & CMS management and has experience working with:

Josh is a positive person who approaches problems thoughtfully and collaboratively. If he’s not online, he’s probably listening to music or out on a bike ride.
